Executive Summary

DigitalNurse is a comprehensive health management platform combining a Flutter mobile app for patients and caregivers with a React/TypeScript web portal for administrative oversight.

The platform enables seamless care coordination, medication management, health tracking, and administrative oversight in a unified, HIPAA-compliant ecosystem.
